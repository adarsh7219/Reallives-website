Empathy
Ecology / Sustainability
RealLives nurturing social-emotional abilities.
We can nudge young minds from self-immersion to imagination, to possibilities, to asking questions, to making virtual life-choices, associating responses with UNs Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and 'experiencing' their effects. We can leverage their receptivity to screen experience to develop and strengthen non-cognitive skills like creativity, collaboration, problem solving, grit and self-regulation.
